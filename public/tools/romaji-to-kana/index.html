Frequently Asked Questions

What is Romaji?

Romaji is the romanization of the Japanese language — writing Japanese phonetics using English letters, e.g. "konnichiwa" or "arigatou".

What is the difference between Hiragana and Katakana?

Hiragana is used for native Japanese words and grammar. Katakana is used mainly for foreign loanwords, technical terms, and emphasis.

How do I convert Romaji to Hiragana?
