PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April 22, 2026  |  Effective: April 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

ZenithTools ("we", "us", "our") operates the website zenithtools.vercel.app (the "Site"), a collection of free browser-based utility tools. Our contact email is privacy@zenithtools.com.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in radical privacy. Here is what actually happens with your data:

  • Tool Inputs: All tool inputs (recipes, words, text, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. This data is never sent to our servers and is discarded when you leave the page.
  • IP Address: Our hosting provider (Vercel) may log your IP address for security and abuse prevention purposes, as is standard for all web hosting.
  • Usage Analytics: We may use anonymized, aggregate analytics (e.g. page view counts) to understand which tools are popular. No personal identifiers are collected.
  • No Accounts: We do not have a login system. We do not collect your email address, name, or any personal details.

3. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies. You can manage your preferences via our Cookie Policy page.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (e.g. storing your cookie consent preference).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These do not identify individuals.
  • Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): If you have consented to advertising, Google AdSense may place cookies to serve relevant advertisements. Google's advertising cookies (DoubleClick) allow Google and its partners to show you ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet. For more information, see Google's Advertising Policy. You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

ZenithTools uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads on our Site based on prior visits to this and other sites.

  • Google may use the advertising cookie to serve ads to users based on their interest and prior site visits.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings or via AboutAds.info.
  • We comply with Google's AdSense Program Policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our tools or site infrastructure may interface with the following third parties:

  • Vercel — Our hosting provider. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Used for typography (no personal data collected). Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising platform. Google Privacy Policy
  • ipapi.co / ipinfo.io — Used by the Network Inspector tool to look up IP information. Your IP is sent to their API. They do not store it for our purposes.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data. (Since we hold essentially none, this is trivially satisfied.)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for advertising purposes. Withdraw consent via our cookie banner at any time.
  • California (CCPA): California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, sold, or disclosed. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zenithtools.com.

7. Data Retention

Because we do not store your tool inputs or personal data, there is nothing to retain. Server logs maintained by Vercel (our hosting provider) are automatically deleted per their standard retention policy (typically 30 days).

8. Children's Privacy

ZenithTools does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 for users in the EU). If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact privacy@zenithtools.com immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@zenithtools.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.
