How to Use Recipe Scaler Pro

Recipe Scaler Pro lets you multiply or divide any recipe instantly. Cooking for a crowd of 20? Halving a recipe for two? Just paste your ingredients, set the scale factor, and get perfectly adjusted quantities in seconds.

  • Paste your full ingredients list into the text box — one ingredient per line
  • Set the scale factor (e.g. 2 to double, 0.5 to halve, 3 to triple)
  • Click Scale Recipe to see adjusted quantities instantly
  • Use the Quick Convert panel to check US Imperial vs Metric equivalents
  • Click Copy to copy all scaled ingredients to your clipboard

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scale a recipe?

Paste your ingredients list, set your scale factor (e.g. 2x for double), and click Scale Recipe. The tool handles all the math automatically.

Can I convert cups to grams?

Yes, Recipe Scaler Pro supports conversion between US Imperial and Metric units including cups, tablespoons, ounces, grams, and milliliters.

Can I scale down a recipe to fewer servings?

Absolutely. Set a scale factor less than 1 (e.g. 0.5 for half) to reduce any recipe to smaller portions.

Is the recipe scaler free?

Yes, Recipe Scaler Pro is completely free to use. No account or login required — it runs entirely in your browser.

About the Free Recipe Scaler

The DokiDokiTools Recipe Scaler is a free online recipe multiplier that instantly scales any recipe up or down to any number of servings. Whether you are doubling a batch of cookies for a party, halving a soup recipe for one, or tripling a casserole for a crowd, this tool handles all the ingredient math automatically.

The recipe converter also supports US Customary to Metric unit conversion — converting cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, and ounces to grams, milliliters, and litres. This makes it ideal for international recipes where measurements differ, or for bakers who prefer weighing ingredients for precision.

Everything runs in your browser. Your ingredient list is never sent to a server. The recipe scaler works on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop — with no installation and no login required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I scale a recipe by a custom amount?

Yes. Enter any target serving count — not just whole numbers. You can scale to 1.5x, 2.5x, or any other multiplier by setting the appropriate servings.

Does it work for baking recipes?

Yes. The scaler works for any recipe including baked goods. Note that baking chemistry can be sensitive to large scale changes — results above 3x or below 0.5x may need minor manual adjustments.
