How to Use Recipe Scaler Pro

Recipe Scaler Pro lets you multiply or divide any recipe instantly. Cooking for a crowd of 20? Halving a recipe for two? Just paste your ingredients, set the scale factor, and get perfectly adjusted quantities in seconds.

  • Paste your full ingredients list into the text box — one ingredient per line
  • Set the scale factor (e.g. 2 to double, 0.5 to halve, 3 to triple)
  • Click Scale Recipe to see adjusted quantities instantly
  • Use the Quick Convert panel to check US Imperial vs Metric equivalents
  • Click Copy to copy all scaled ingredients to your clipboard

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scale a recipe?

Paste your ingredients list, set your scale factor (e.g. 2x for double), and click Scale Recipe. The tool handles all the math automatically.

Can I convert cups to grams?

Yes, Recipe Scaler Pro supports conversion between US Imperial and Metric units including cups, tablespoons, ounces, grams, and milliliters.

Can I scale down a recipe to fewer servings?

Absolutely. Set a scale factor less than 1 (e.g. 0.5 for half) to reduce any recipe to smaller portions.

Is the recipe scaler free?

Yes, Recipe Scaler Pro is completely free to use. No account or login required — it runs entirely in your browser.
